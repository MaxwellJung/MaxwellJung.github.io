UCSB ECE153B Final Project
Digital Camera


Maxwell Jung and Baron Young



See pull requests for progress updates


Overview

Create a digital camera capable of capturing and saving a photograph. A pre-made camera module will capture the image, and TFT LCD will preview the captured image in real time. The user can press a button (either on the touchscreen LCD, pushbutton on Nucleo board, or clicking rotary encoder) to save the previewed image to the microSD card.
